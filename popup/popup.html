Why use this extension?
The Honey Barrel helps you find the best whisky and wine deals by automatically comparing prices on product pages with the BAXUS marketplace. Save time, discover better offers, and never miss a great deal!
How it works: Just browse your favorite whisky or wine shop. If a better price is found on BAXUS, you'll see a popup with the comparison.
